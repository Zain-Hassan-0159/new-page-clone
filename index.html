    Kevin OLeary: AI powered trading bots, the biggest opportunity of the century for everyday people.

    Billionaire investor and Shark Tank co-host buys 51% of AlgoBot Tech, launches in Canda.

    By Danile Johnson Fox News
    Published August 24, 2024 9:49pm EDT

    OLeary says AI trading bots are the ultimate wealth-building tool, shaping the new era of wealth.

    In a recent episode of Shark Tank, Kevin O'Leary swooped in and secured what hes calling the deal of a lifetime, right from under Mark Cuban's nose. The tension was palpable as O'Leary outmaneuvered Cuban with a last-minute move.

    Kevin O'Leary, known for his sharp business acumen and strategic investments, recognized a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity when he encountered Alexis Grayson and his groundbreaking AI technology.

    At just 25 years old, Grayson had achieved the extraordinarydeveloping an algorithm capable of predicting market behavior with over 90% accuracy for short-term intervals of 15, 30, and 60 minutes.

    This kind of precision was unheard of, even among the most advanced AI experts, I knew instantly that this was more than just a promising startup; it was a goldmine that could redefine the financial landscape. Says OLeary.

    Thats why O'Leary was willing to pay whatever it took to secure 51% of the company, ensuring control over a technology that could potentially dominate the marketAlexis Grayson had cracked the code, and he wasnt about to let this prodigy slip through his fingers.

    STRANGE ACCIDENT CREATED THE MOST PROFITABLE NEXT-GEN AI TRADING BOT

    AI and machine learning have revolutionized market trading by enabling algorithms to analyze vast amounts of data at lightning speed, and make predictions with unprecedented accuracy.

    Sometimes, the biggest breakthroughs happen by accident. In 1895, Wilhelm Conrad Roentgen accidentally discovered X-rays while experimenting with cathode rays. Says OLeary.

    Its amazing how unexpected events can lead to incredible discoveries that change the world. Thats exactly how AlgoBot was born.

    During a routine test of their AI model, a power surge caused the system to malfunction. At first, they were worried it would be a setback, but what happened next was beyond anything they expected.

    The AI, already trained on a vast amount of data, started processing information in a new, highly accurate way that wasnt planned at all.

    The power surge made the AI combine different parts of its system in a new way, creating a model that could remember both recent and older information at the same time.

    This change allowed the AI to notice small price patterns and trends that were impossible to see before, giving its accuracy and profitability a huge boost.

    BIGGEST OPPORTUNITY OF OUR LIFETIME. REMEMBER HOW BITCOIN SKYROCKETED FROM 15 CENTS TO AROUND $60,000?

    "OLeary is very aggressive in pursuing technologies that use AI. He believes that, when it comes to trading, AI will rewrite history.

    We are living in a crazy reality. Its easy to become insanely wealthy in a short time, as long as you accept the new reality we are in and take action, says OLeary.

    In the old economy, it took years of hard work to become wealthy. Today, wealth is about recognizing monster opportunities when they come along. And AI-powered trading bots are the ultimate of these monster opportunities, says Kevin OLeary.

    Just stop and think about this for a moment. What is profitable trading all about? It's simply speculating accurately where prices will be in the near future. And that's exactly what AlgoBot achieves with remarkable precisionit almost seems like magic! OLeary says.

    IN TRADING, WHAT MATTERS ARE RESULTS. ALGOBOTS RESULTS SPEAK FOR THEMSELVES.

    Kevin OLeary calls his AlgoBot Shark Tank deal the best deal he has ever made on the show.

    If you recall, the competition was fierce! Everyone wanted in on this AlgoBot deal, says OLeary.

    What viewers never saw was the behind the scenes of what was going on that day. Alexis, one of the co-founders of AlgoBot, offered to fire up the bot on a small $250 account for 60 minutes, according to OLeary.

    The bot took 11 trades, resulting in only one loss. That small $250 account grew to $619 within one hour.

    OLeary says that what impressed him most was the ability of the bot to minimize losses.

    Look, what matters most at the end of the day is protecting your money, he said. OLeary says that before releasing it to the public, they tested AlgoBot on over 100 accounts. It amazed me how the bot got out of losing trades almost instantly, protecting the users capital, he told me.

    $819 AVERAGE DAILY PROFIT PER USER. JUST INSTALL THE BOT, SET IT, AND FORGET IT.

    OLeary says this is the biggest opportunity for everyday people. Even bigger than bitcoin or anything else weve seen in the past 20 years. (Credit: Shark Tank)

    Canada is the second country the bot has been released in. Demand has been explosive says OLeary, Our website crashed two times!

    There are two options for using AlgoBot. You can purchase a master license, which allows you to trade the bot on as many accounts as you wish. The price is $15,000.

    Or, you can use AlgoBot for free, but only on one account. This is the best option if you are trading just for yourself. This option requires a deposit of $250 into a trading account. This money will be used by the bot to trade for you and you can withdraw it whenever you wish.

    The good thing is, youre not really risking $250 (thats just the minimum deposit), because the bot can start trading with just $20, says OLeary.

    To date, 6,823 Canadians have joined AlgoBot, with the average daily profit per user standing at a whopping $819, according to OLeary.

    How can you join AlgoBot? Due to the high demand, registrations are often closed. After submitting a short form, an AlgoBot specialist will call you for a brief two-minute interview.
